Welcome!

Welcome to Capital Gain at CapitalTech.us We hope you will enjoy playing this with your friends and family. This was developed with the intent to enable you to have a great game night with family or friends even if the world takes you far from home. The entire family can play around a board using a tablet at home while other members who are not present can join in on their laptops.

This is a game of investment and acquisition. You build organizations, grow them, and seek to merge them to make a bonus.
